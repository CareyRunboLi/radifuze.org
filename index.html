RDFZ Science Journal (RSJ)

Welcome to our Science Journal — a collaborative creation brought to life by six student-led clubs: iChemistry, the Biology Club, the Math Club, the uniquely imaginative Sleep Club, the Technology Dream Workshop (ICC), and RDFZ Physics.

Who can submit?

Any student. Any level. Any idea.

We encourage everyone—from budding researchers to curious dreamers—to contribute.

What makes our journal special?

We welcome all kinds of science-related writing — whether it’s academic, creative, experimental, or even humorous.

Articles can be written in English or Chinese.

You don’t need to be a science genius or top of your class — if you have an idea, a question, or an observation about the world, we want to hear it.
